Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you might want to know before you start your 200 days.

Do I need any German before I join?

No prior German is needed. The journey starts at A1 on day one — complete beginners are exactly who the 200-day roadmap is built for, and Gluck Buddy helps you practise speaking from the very first lesson.

How does the 100% job guarantee work?

As you reach the required language level, curated openings from our German employer partners appear in your portal, and our team supports you through interviews and placement. The guarantee is backed by a written agreement when you enrol.

Are classes live or recorded?

Both. You join daily live classes with expert teachers, and every session is recorded in HD so you can rewatch anytime. Your attendance and watch progress are tracked automatically in the portal.

Do you handle the visa process too?

Yes. You upload your documents once into a secure vault, and we guide your application through every stage — verification, filing, interview prep and approval — with live status updates so you always know where things stand.

Can I learn from outside Sri Lanka?

Absolutely. The entire experience — live classes, exercises, Gluck Buddy, GlückArena and your progress dashboard — runs in the portal from any device, anywhere with an internet connection.
